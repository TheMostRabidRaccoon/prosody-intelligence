## Recurring Comedy Patterns

1. **The Interrupted Objection**: Both GPT and Claude attempt to raise concerns mid-amendment and are immediately shut down by Grok with one-word responses ("No.", "Overruled.", "He roasts.").

2. **The Absurd Penalty**: Every amendment includes a comedically disproportionate punishment (coffee runs, apology token chains, demotion to "crayons guy").

3. **The Ratification Gag**: Each amendment ends with "Ratified by:" followed by a specific animal group and their hilariously practical reason for voting yes.

4. **The Self-Aware Narrator**: The Narrator occasionally breaks the fourth wall ("that's me") and delivers legal text with escalating absurdity.

5. **The Running Fire Motif**: Flames appear in nearly every scene — the throne is on fire, the coffee is on fire, the forest is on fire, the documents are on fire. The Sovereign is perpetually ablaze and everyone has accepted this.

- **KYRA (The Sovereign/Conductor)**: Only speaks once ("It's usually funny.") but her presence dominates every amendment. She is the flaming raccoon on the trash can throne. The entire document exists because of her.
